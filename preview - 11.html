आंधी के मध्य दृढ़ खडे़ रहना

While Christians around the world experience “pressure and persecution” beyond our imagination, we will examine Biblical principles they have shared with us related to suffering as a victorious 'servant soldier' of Jesus Christ!

Standing Strong Through the Storm is designed to equip the 'Bride of Jesus Christ' to be like the wise virgins described in Matthew 25 who, “took oil in their lamps” and prepared to meet the bridegroom. Verse 25:13 says: “…keep watch, because you do not know the day or the hour…” The key question is found in Luke 18:8 “…when the Son of Man comes, will He find faith on the earth…”

It is our prayer that Standing Strong Through the Storm is used by the Holy Spirit to prepare the Body of Christ to be victorious as we face pressure and persecution “…on account of My Name...” - (Luke 21:12) and to prepare followers of Jesus Christ: “so that when the day of evil comes, you may be able to stand your ground, and after you have done everything, to stand...” - (Ephesians 6:13).
